01 — Problem

The Challenge

Art discovery platforms frequently present historical knowledge in fragmented, dense, or inaccessible ways. Users struggle to connect artworks with context, narrative, and learning experiences.

The challenge was to design a mobile experience that balances education, discoverability, storytelling, and usability while making cultural exploration feel approachable.

  • Information overload
  • Weak discovery mechanisms
  • Limited contextual learning
  • Navigation complexity
  • Need for engaging educational interaction
